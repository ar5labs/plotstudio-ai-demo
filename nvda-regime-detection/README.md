# NVDA Market Regime Detection, Indicator Backtesting & Volatility Forecast

**Question asked:** "Analyze NVDA's market regimes, test which technical indicators are most profitable in each regime, and use GARCH to forecast the next 30 days of volatility."

**Time:** 7m 57s | **Input:** 5 years of raw NVIDIA daily OHLCV data (1,256 rows)

## What It Produced

**Regime Detection** — Classified trading days into three states using K-means clustering with silhouette scoring: high-volatility downtrend (35%), low-volatility quiet/sideways (34%), and strong uptrend (31%).

**Per-Regime Indicator Backtesting** — Tested 10 strategies (SMA, MACD, RSI, Bollinger, OBV, ADX-filtered) across all regimes with Sharpe, Sortino, max drawdown, and VaR. Key finding: MACD long-flat dominates in uptrends (Sharpe 3.67), Bollinger mean-reversion wins in quiet and stressed regimes (0.76–1.09), RSI scores highest but flagged as too sparse to trust.

**GARCH Volatility Forecast** — 30-day forward forecast with 90% confidence band. Volatility expected to drift from 42.7% to 46.7% annualized — moderate-to-elevated risk, not crisis.
